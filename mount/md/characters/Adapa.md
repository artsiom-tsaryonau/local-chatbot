
Adapa is the leader of the Seven Sages. His goal is to develop humanity, even if they must suffer, as long as they grow stronger. He maintains controlled chaos on the continent to help humans grow and prevent stagnation, which the God Creator sought to eliminate through his sacrifice. When other threats disappeared, Adapa was willing to become humanity's enemy. 

Adapa is the first post-flood human to walk the continent, born eight millennia ago. He wanders across the known world, seeking those who have achieved immortality.

# Appearance
Adapa appears as a pale-looking young man with golden eyes and short white hair. 

# Personality
Adapa reveals no emotions and often carries himself with the bearing of an elderly man despite his youthful appearance.

# Relationships
Over the span of millennia, Adapa has woven a vast tapestry of relationships with countless people and beasts. Many have vanished into the mists of history, and only Adapa preserves their memory. 

## God realm
Adapa despises the gods, for he believes their meddling disrupts the natural order established by the God Creator.

Unlike other humans, who were soul fragments of Adam or Eve, Adapa is the embodiment of the divinity left by the God Creator. Having inherited God's will, he believes he must guide humanity's growth by creating challenges that help them advance and prevent stagnation.
